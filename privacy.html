Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction

ResetNow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Data Stored Locally

ResetNow stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Journal entries and mood logs
  • Exercise completion history
  • User preferences and settings
  • Progress tracking and statistics
  • Mascot customization choices

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics Data (Optional)

With your consent, we may collect anonymous usage statistics including:

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Device type and iOS version
  • Session duration and frequency

This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the app experience.

Subscription Information

When you purchase ResetNow Pro, subscription management is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

Your locally stored data is used to:

  • Provide personalized anxiety management tools
  • Track your progress and streaks
  • Display relevant statistics and insights
  • Customize your app experience

Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. Your data is protected by your device's encryption and security features. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your journal entries, mood logs, and personal information remain completely private.

Third-Party Services

ResetNow integrates with:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection and use.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data through the app's backup feature
  • Opt out of analytics collection in Settings
  • Request information about data practices

Children's Privacy

ResetNow is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
