Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using ResetNow?

Download the app and complete onboarding. You'll have immediate access to free features including 3-minute resets, check-ins, and basic breathing exercises.

What's included in ResetNow Pro?

  • Full breathing exercise library
  • All visualization programs
  • Complete sleep audio library
  • All Play games
  • Full Learn lessons & journeys
  • Advanced statistics (30-90 days)
  • Extra mascot cosmetics

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  • Open iPhone Settings
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Select "Subscriptions"
  • Find "ResetNow Pro"

How do I cancel my subscription?

iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → ResetNow Pro → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open ResetNow → Settings → Tap "Restore Purchases"

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! All your personal data stays on your device. We don't collect or store your journal entries, mood data, or personal information.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

  • Force quit and restart the app
  • Check for updates in the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If issues persist, contact us below
