Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using Reset?

Download the app and complete onboarding. You'll have immediate access to free features including 3-minute resets, check-ins, and basic breathing exercises.

What's included in Reset Pro?

  • Full breathing exercise library
  • All visualization programs
  • Complete sleep audio library
  • All Play games
  • Advanced statistics (30-90 days)
  • Extra mascot cosmetics

How do I manage my subscription?

Open iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Find "Reset Pro"

How do I cancel?

iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Reset Pro → Cancel Subscription

Can I get a refund?

Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and request a refund.

How do I restore purchases?

Open Reset → Settings → Tap "Restore Purchases"

Is my data private?

Yes! All data stays on your device. We don't collect or store your personal information.
